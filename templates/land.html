Hospital Management System

Welcome to Hospital Management System

Your health, our priority. Manage appointments, patient records, and staff interactions seamlessly through our secure platform.

Access for All Roles

  • Doctor: View appointments, patient details, and update availability.
  • Patient: Book appointments, view medical history, and manage profile.

System Highlights

  • Secure login for Admin, Doctor, and Patient
  • Real-time appointment scheduling
  • Hospital-doctor mapping with one-to-many relationships
  • Patient registration with age auto-calculation from DOB
  • Flash messaging for login feedback and alerts
  • Session-based access control and logout

How It Works

  1. Register as a patient or doctor
  2. Login securely with your credentials
  3. Patients can book appointments with available doctors
  4. Doctors manage their schedules and view patient details
  5. Admins oversee hospital data and system integrity
